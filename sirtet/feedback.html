Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Sirtet?

Sirtet is a match-4 puzzle game. Insert rows from the bottom and select tetromino shapes to clear cells of the same color. Combine matches to create combos and boost your score!

What devices are supported?

iOS 16.0 or later, compatible with iPhone and iPad.

Is my progress saved?

Yes, your game progress and high scores are saved locally on your device.

Is there iCloud sync?

Not currently, but it's on our roadmap for a future update.

Where can I find other players?

Join our Discord community at https://discord.gg/MMb5kdHJ to connect with other Sirtet players, share strategies, and participate in challenges!

  • iPhone and iPad compatible
  • Approximately 50 MB of storage space
